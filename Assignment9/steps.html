SUPPLIES

  • Flowers! Can be any kind. The less bulky the receptacle (the part where the pedals and flower meet)
  • Two 4" x 4" square pieces of wood. Anything thicker than a 1/2" will do.
  • Two 4" x 4" pieces of card board
  • Two 4" x 4" pieces of paper
  • Two medium sized clamps
  • Plastic sleeve or small container to hold the pressed flowers once they are complete

OPTIONAL SUPPLIES (For a little extra extra)

  • Epoxy kit and silicone molds. If you would like to make jewelry or trinkets.
  • Glass sandwich frame. If you would like to create artwork you can hang!

STEP BY STEP (Day by Day)

  1. Cut your flowers! Make sure they are flat because they will press better
  2. place on a piece of wood, cardboard, and paper combination. Items must be in that order.
  3. Place the other set of wood, cardboard, paper on top. Make sure it is even and everythin aligned.
  4. Take each clamp and clamp down on each side. Wait 5-7 days.
  5. YOU DID IT! take off the clamps and see your creations! Next you can use mold and epoxy to create jewerly or other trinkets!
